NusantaraVerse is a Virtual Reality-Based Game that presents Indonesian culture to introduce to the world and preserve the culture.
NusantaraVerse provides various Indonesian cultural content from various tribes in Indonesia, such as traditional houses, traditional clothes, traditional food, traditional musical instruments, traditional weapons and traditional dances.
